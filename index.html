🔍 What is WiFi Heatmapping?

WiFi heatmapping is a visual survey that shows how strong (or weak) your wireless signal is throughout a space. We use pro tools to find:

  • 🔴 Dead zones and weak spots
  • 🟢 Areas with strong coverage
  • ⚠️ Interference or overlapping access points

With this data, we help you optimise your network layout for best performance.
